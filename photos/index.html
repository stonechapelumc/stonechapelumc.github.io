Historic Photos

More than two centuries of worship on one piece of ground leaves a long trail of memories. We are gathering photographs from across the years, so this page will grow as those images are scanned and added.

Have an old photo of Stone Chapel? We would love to include it. Photographs of the building through the years, past congregations, weddings, baptisms, and church events are all welcome. Please get in touch and we will arrange to scan and return your original.
